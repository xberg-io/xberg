POLL RESULTS OF THE ANNUAL GENERAL MEETING
HELD ON 30 JUNE 2021
The Board is pleased to announce that all the resolutions proposed at the AGM as set out in the Notice
of AGM were duly passed by the Shareholders by way of poll at the AGM.
The board (the “Board”) of directors (“Directors”) of Kaisun Holdings Limited (the “Company”) is
pleased to announce the poll results of the annual general meeting of the Company held on 30 June 2021
(the “AGM”).
As at the date of the AGM, the total number of issued shares of the Company was 576,566,055 shares,
which was the total number of shares entitling the holders to attend and vote for or against any of the
resolutions proposed at the AGM. There were no restrictions on any shareholders to cast votes on any of
the proposed resolutions at the AGM.
Computershare Hong Kong Investor Services Limited, the share registrar of the Company in Hong
