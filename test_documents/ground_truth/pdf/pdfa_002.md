# Bulk and Regional Tariff Design Stakeholder Engagement
# Session 5B (DOS) hosted on May 20, 2021

## I. Purpose and objectives of the session
The purpose of this session is to engage stakeholders in a discussion of the AESO's demand opportunity
service (DOS) modernization recommendation to allow expanded eligibility (e.g., energy storage).

The session objectives include:
- Present additional background on the AESO's demand opportunity service – what it is, the service it
provides, and how it operates today
- Share our learnings on DOS modernization stakeholder feedback
- Present and discuss the AESO's DOS modernization recommendation for the purpose of seeking
takeholder feedback on the recommendation
