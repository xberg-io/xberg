Government Information (Public Access) Act – Annual Report for Agency Griffith City Council
Clause 7A: Details of the review carried out by the agency under section 7 (3) of the Act during the reporting year and the details
of any information made publicly available by the agency as a result of the review
Reviews carried out by the agency Information made publicly available by the agency
Yes Yes
Review of information held by Council that may be of interest to the public (information that is not already released proactively).
Review GIPA applications both formal and information that have been received by Council to gauge what categories of
information are requested. Regular review the initiatives, developments and projects that Council feels the public would like to
be informed of. During the reporting period, we reviewed this program by: Yearly review of Council’s Information Guide,
endorsed by the General Manager and Senior Staff, reported to and endorsed by Council. Liaise regularly with senior
management & the Corporate Communications Department in relation to the release of information via the media (radio, TV &
newspaper), community engagement (consultation sessions, letters, media release, newsletters etc), website, & Council’s
social media platforms (Facebook). Review of Disclosure Log to identify patterns & themes in the type of information sought.
Staff consult with the Public Officer in relation to the type of information that is requested & the information that can be
proactively released. As a result of this review, we released the following information proactively: Main Drain J Floodplain Risk
Study and Plan Griffith Regional Aquatic Centre Masterplan CBD Strategy Internal Audit Reports Crime Prevention Plan
Griffith City Pound Statistics and Activities Community Services Directory Community Catchup
