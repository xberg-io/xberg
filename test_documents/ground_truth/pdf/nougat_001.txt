The heart is an important organ that keeps the blood moving
throughout the body. Blood brings important nutrients to all body
systems, including the brain. The brain needs oxygen and glucose from
blood to function. What happens when the blood supply in the brain
changes? The result may be a stroke. In this lesson, you will learn about
what a stroke is, what the risk factors are, and ways to prevent stroke.
