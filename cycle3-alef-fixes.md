We already added `<LangVersion>13</LangVersion>` to the hand-written `Kreuzberg.csproj` (which wins over
Directory.Build.props for that project), but for consistency the alef scaffold should be updated:

- Alef source: `alef-scaffold/languages/csharp.rs` (or wherever Directory.Build.props template lives)
- Change `LangVersion` from `latest` to `13`
- Or make it configurable via `[crates.csharp]` in `alef.toml`
